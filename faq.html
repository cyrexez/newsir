FAQ

What is the expected income from SIR?

Depending on the time you put in, for 40 hours you can earn as much as 40K

What do i have to get as benfits?

We will provide access to information, educational resources, scholarships and fellowship.

How do I opt out of the program?

You are required to give a months notice and pay all outstanding loans.

How long does it take to access these benefits?

You are required to be a fellow of the program,which takes about 3 to 6 months

Do you need collateral to get a loan?

No, you don't need collateral to acquire a loan, you only need a fellow from Scholar in Residence to act as a guarrantor.

How much can I take as loan?

Loans delivered are in a progression

  • 6 months: N50,000
  • Over a year: N100,000

This however is subject to review

How do I get loans to buy educational materials, books, laptops and phones?

Depending on location....

  • In Nigeria, 50% of the cost will be covered by us and all information regarding the materials in question are expected to be sent to us and the materials shall be gotten sent to you from us.
  • In the US, 70% of the cost will be covered by us and all information regarding the materials in question are expected to be sent to us and the materials shall be gotten sent to you from us
